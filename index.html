We catered many weddings and were featured in a prominent Arlington newspaper for our culinary twist. Over the years I have had a large interest in the web development field. My huge desire to learn and relentless approach to full stack developement will only prove to be more and more valuable with each passing day. I look forward to seeing what this field has in store for me as I continue on this journey to become a better version of myself on a daily basis.
